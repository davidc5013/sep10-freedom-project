What is this project about

Fitness and technology came a long way from where we started. From free weights to wearable accessories to track our heartbeats and websites and apps that allow use a personalized workouts and food. whether your old fashion on working out or want more technology into it, you'll find out in this project.

Software

Apple Fitness+

Apple fitness is subcription-based fitness app that offers a range of workout plans, helps you be in condition and is part of the apple ecosystem like the Iphone, Ipad, Macbook and the apple watch.

PRO:

  • It offers classes by professional trainers
  • it gives your personal workouts based on your physical journey
  • You can workout from your house if you don't have access to a gym
  • Offers a playlist with bangers and well known artist while working out
  • It also offers personal and expert gudiance

    • CONS:

    While it has its pros, like everything it has its flaws such as:

  • You need a apple device to have access to the app
  • Since its subcripition-based, the money does add up over time
  • Its not related nutrition based so it doesn't have anything with diet plans or nutrition
  • Compared to other fitness apps its not wide-range like other apps.

    • Its pros and cons are somewhat balance but if you have devices of apple ecosytem, then i recommend this app.

    Planet Fitness App

    The planet fitness app is a free-based app that allow its users get workout whild at the gym or at home.

    PROS:

  • Theres a crowd meter in the app so you can know how busy it is when working out
  • You can enter to different planet fitness gyms
  • Theres equipment tutrotals and content to help guide you
  • There is exclusive member perks
  • Different workouts depeneding on your fitness journey

    • CONS:

  • There is a lack of live coaching
  • Sometimes there is issues with loging in
  • Most of the usage is in a gym
  • If you don't have a memebership then theres no use within the app
  • Limited intergration

    • I would recommend this app as long as you have a memebership with PF gym!
